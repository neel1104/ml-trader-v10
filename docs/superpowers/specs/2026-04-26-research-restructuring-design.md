# Design Doc: Restructuring research.md

**Date:** 2026-04-26
**Topic:** Document Restructuring for Quantitative Crypto Trading Research

## Goal
Transform the raw text content of `research.md` into a professionally formatted Markdown document with clear hierarchy, tables, and mathematical notation.

## Proposed Structure
- **Title (H1):** Quantitative Frameworks for Cryptocurrency Trading
- **Executive Summary/Introduction:** First paragraph.
- **Section 1 (H2):** Market Dynamics and Liquidity Profiles (includes Asset Pair table)
- **Section 2 (H2):** Machine Learning Architectures (includes sub-sections for TFT, DRL, Hybrid models and a summary table)
- **Section 3 (H2):** High-Dimensional Feature Engineering (includes sub-sections for On-Chain, Microstructure, and Sentiment)
- **Section 4 (H2):** Market-Neutral Strategies (includes sub-sections for Funding Arb, Stat-Arb, Basis Trading and a summary table)
- **Section 5 (H2):** Optimization Metrics (includes Risk-Adjusted Ratios table and Advanced Targets)
- **Section 6 (H2):** Validation and Hyperparameter Tuning (includes CPCV, WFO, and Optimization methods)
- **Section 7 (H2):** Infrastructure and Agentic Paradigms
- **Section 8 (H2):** Synthesis/Roadmap (Phase-based breakdown)

## Formatting Details
- **Tables:** Standard Markdown pipe tables for all comparison data.
- **Math:** LaTeX blocks for formulas like WFE.
- **Hierarchy:** Strict H1 -> H2 -> H3 nesting.
- **Emphasis:** Bold for key terms and metrics.

## Success Criteria
- Valid Markdown syntax.
- No lost content from original file.
- Significantly improved readability and navigation.
